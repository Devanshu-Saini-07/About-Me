# About Me
🌐 Overview
Your site is a personal portfolio webpage designed to introduce yourself, showcase your skills, and provide ways for people to connect with you. It’s structured into clear sections — About, Social Links, Other Handles, Non-Professional Handles, Skills, and Contact — each styled to look like modern cards with shadows, spacing, and hover effects. The design is clean, minimal, and professional, while still reflecting your personality.

🔹 Key Features
- About Section
- Displays your name, profile image, and a short introduction.
- The image is centered and styled as a circular avatar, giving a modern look.
- The text highlights your passion for coding, gaming, and learning new things.
- Social Media Links
- Organized list of professional platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, WhatsApp, and Instagram.
- Each link opens in a new tab (target="_blank"), ensuring visitors don’t lose your page.
- Styled with hover effects to make them interactive.
- Other Handles
- Includes your LeetCode profile, showing your coding practice and problem-solving skills.
- Adds credibility by linking to platforms where you actively learn and contribute.
- Non-Professional Handles
- Showcases your creative side with Instagram, YouTube channels, Discord, and Telegram.
- Highlights hobbies like gaming and anime edits, making the page more personal and relatable.
- Skills Section
- Lists your technical skills: C++, HTML & CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, Git & GitHub.
- This section acts as a quick resume snapshot for recruiters or collaborators.
- Contact Section
- Simple form with fields for name and message.
- Uses mailto: so messages can be sent directly to your email.
- Clean design with input fields styled for usability.
- Styling & Effects (CSS)
- Navbar: Fixed at the top, with hover and active states.
- Banner: Bold header introducing your portfolio.
- Cards: Each section styled with background, padding, rounded corners, and shadows.
- Hover Effects: Links underline on hover, product-like cards scale slightly when hovered.
- Footer: Professional closing with copyright.

🔹 User Experience
- Navigation: Easy to move between sections using the navbar.
- Readability: Clean fonts, spacing, and card layout make content easy to scan.
- Interactivity: Hover effects, clickable links, and form submission add engagement.
- Responsiveness: With CSS flex/grid, the layout can adapt to mobile screens.

🔹 Why It Works
- It balances professional presentation (skills, projects, social links) with personal touch (gaming, edits, hobbies).
- Recruiters see your coding background, while friends and followers connect with your creative side.
- The design is minimal but modern, with effects that make it feel polished without being overwhelming.

👉 In short, this portfolio site is a digital identity card for you: it introduces who you are, what you do, what you’ve built, and how people can connect with you — all wrapped in a clean, interactive design.
